•	Toggle it on with the button you made
•	Close your hand with a fist to move the coin to your thumb
•	Thumbs up to flick it up, it will land on either heads or tails randomly
•	Close your hand again to move it up to be ready to be flicked
•	Point with your finger to flick it to the other hand
•	Open *both* hands to drop it in world
•	Close hand to grab it again
•	Rocker gesture to throw it away (it will toggle itself off after this)
